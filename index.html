FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Nexora. Can't find an answer? Reach out to us directly.

Contact Us
What types of videos does Nexora produce?
We produce brand films, ad films, social media reels, product videos, corporate videos, AI animations, motion graphics, and documentary-style content. Each project is tailored to your brand's needs and target platform.
How does AI fit into your production process?
We use AI tools for tasks like script assistance, generative animations, automated editing, color grading, and VFX compositing. This hybrid approach means you get cinema-quality results faster and more cost-effectively—without sacrificing creative control.
What is the typical turnaround time?
It depends on the project scope. Social media reels can be delivered in 3–5 working days, brand films in 1–2 weeks, and larger ad film projects in 2–4 weeks. We always agree on a timeline before starting.
Do you work with clients outside Ahmedabad?
Absolutely! While we're based in Ahmedabad, we work with clients across India and internationally. For outstation projects, we travel for shoots and handle everything else remotely through seamless online collaboration.
How much does a project cost?
Every project is unique, so we provide custom quotes based on your requirements. Social media content packages start affordably, while full-scale brand films and ad films are quoted individually. Contact us for a free consultation and estimate.
